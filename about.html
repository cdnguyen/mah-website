About

I was born and raised in Charlotte, North Carolina. I attended the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, where I earned a degree in Graphic Design. After graduating, I lived in Baltimore, Maryland for a few years, but I had a strong desire to live somewhere outside of the United States. After years of searching, I found an opportunity to live in Chambéry, France.

I am now a product designer for Leadformance, a company located in Chambery. We design and create software to help brands attract more customers to their stores.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you can find me somewhere in the mountains or sky.
