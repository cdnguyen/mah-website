About

A producer originating from New York City and now settling in Sydney, Australia DLake (pronounced Dee Lake) has been crafting his ALTERNATIVE URBAN oriented sound since 2002. In that time he's dabbled in and out of a multitude of genres and scenes. From his downtempo/trip hop roots to electro house to club, pop, alt r&b and back again. The near future is looking like the year that all of this genre Infidelity finally pays off.
